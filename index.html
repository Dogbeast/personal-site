My story

Hailing from the mean streets of Renton, located in the bustling environs of Washington state, Daniel divvies up his time amongst new coding challenges, reading material, work, and the occassional game or two. Whereas in the past, Daniel would encounter new languages and attempt to learn a little about them, he now tries to immerse himself in coding languages and methods. Throughout these adventures, he would find new solutions to old problems, new problems to old solutions, and even new problems to old problems. Sometimes drowning in the immensity of it all, and sometimes discovering something new. But always, there would be something else to aim towards and he would find himself amongst the lines of code once more. The only difference between the past and the present...he is no longer the reader, but also the writer.
